Phishing Knowledge Base

Learn how to stay safe in the digital world

  • What is phishing?

    Phishing is a cybercrime where targets are contacted by email, telephone, or text message by someone posing as a legitimate institution to lure individuals into providing sensitive data such as personally identifiable information, banking and credit card details, and passwords.

  • Why does it matter?

    Success Phishing attacks can cause financial loss, identity theft, and compromise entire systems. It relies on establishing false trust with victims.

  • Common Types of Attacks

    Deceptive Phishing: Impersonating legitimate companies.
    Spear Phishing: Targeting specific individuals.
    Smishing: Phishing via SMS.

  • Prevention for Users

    • Choose strong passwords.
    • Be wary of urgent messages.
    • Verify links before clicking.
    • Don't supply personal info via unverified channels.
